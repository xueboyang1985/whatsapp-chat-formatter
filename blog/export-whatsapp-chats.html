Why Export WhatsApp Chats to Computer?

WhatsApp stores your chat history on your phone, but relying on your phone as the sole storage location has risks. Here are the most common reasons people export WhatsApp chats to their computer:

  • Backup and preservation. Phones get lost, damaged, or replaced. Having chat exports on your computer ensures you never lose important conversations.
  • Legal and business records. Chat logs can serve as evidence in disputes, client communication records, or compliance documentation. A formatted export is far more presentable than scrolling through a phone screen.
  • Reading and searching on a larger screen. Reviewing long group chats or project discussions is much easier on a computer monitor with full text search capabilities.
  • Data analysis. Exporting to CSV or JSON allows you to analyze chat patterns, message volume, response times, and more using spreadsheet or data tools.
  • Content creation. Writers, podcasters, and researchers often need to extract quotes or conversation threads from WhatsApp for their work. Formatted exports make citation and organization straightforward.

Export from iPhone (iOS)

Exporting a WhatsApp chat from an iPhone is straightforward. Follow these steps:

  1. Open WhatsApp on your iPhone.
  2. Navigate to the chat or group you want to export.
  3. Tap the contact name or group subject at the top of the chat screen.
  4. Scroll down to the bottom of the contact info page and tap Export Chat.
  5. Choose between:
    • Without Media — Exports only text messages as a .txt file. This is the recommended option for most users because the file is small and contains all the conversation content.
    • With Media — Exports a .zip file containing the text export plus attached photos and videos. Note: the ZIP file can be very large for media-heavy chats.
  6. A share sheet will appear. Select how you want to save or send the file (email, Files app, AirDrop, or cloud storage).
Tip for iPhone users: Use AirDrop to transfer the exported .txt file directly to your Mac wirelessly. Make sure both devices have Bluetooth and Wi-Fi enabled, then select AirDrop from the share sheet and choose your Mac.
Note: WhatsApp on iPhone exports in the [YYYY-MM-DD, HH:MM:SS] Sender: Message format. The tool on this site auto-detects this format, so you don't need to worry about compatibility.

Export from Android

The process on Android is slightly different but equally simple:

  1. Open WhatsApp on your Android phone.
  2. Open the chat or group you want to export.
  3. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top-right corner.
  4. Tap More, then tap Export Chat.
  5. Choose Without Media for a compact text-only export, or With Media for a ZIP file that includes images and videos.
  6. Select how to share the file: via email, Google Drive, Save to Files, or any other sharing option on your device.
Tip for Android users: Google Drive is the most reliable way to transfer the exported file to your computer. Save it to Drive from the share sheet, then download it on your PC or Mac from drive.google.com. No cables needed.
Note: Android exports use the M/D/YY, HH:MM - Sender: Message date format. The formatting tool supports both 12-hour and 24-hour time formats automatically.

Transfer the Chat File to Your Computer

Once you have the exported .txt or .zip file on your phone, you need to get it onto your computer. Here are the most common methods:

Email (easiest for small files)

Select email from the share sheet after exporting. Send the file to your own email address, then download it on your computer. This works well for text-only exports (typically under 1 MB for most chats).

Cloud storage (best for large files)

Save to Google Drive, iCloud Drive, Dropbox, or OneDrive from the share sheet. Then sign in to the same service on your computer and download the file. This method handles both .txt and .zip files without size issues.

USB cable (no internet needed)

Connect your phone to your computer with a USB cable. On iPhone, you may need a third-party file explorer for Windows (macOS uses Finder with the Files app). On Android, the phone appears as an external drive — navigate to WhatsApp/Databases/ or the Downloads folder to find the exported file.

AirDrop (iPhone to Mac)

Select AirDrop from the iOS share sheet, choose your Mac, and the file transfers instantly. This is the fastest method for Apple users.

Messaging apps (quick but temporary)

Send the file to yourself via Telegram, Signal, or a secondary messaging app. Download it on your computer from the web or desktop version of the same app.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I export WhatsApp chats from WhatsApp Web or Desktop?
No. WhatsApp Web and the Desktop app do not have an Export Chat feature. You must export from your phone (iPhone or Android) and then transfer the file to your computer. The phone is the only device that can generate the export file.
Are my chats uploaded to a server when using the formatter?
No. The WhatsApp Chat Formatter runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your chat file never leaves your device. There is no upload, no server, and no signup required. The page even works offline after it has been loaded once.
How do I export a WhatsApp group chat?
The process is identical to exporting a one-on-one chat. Open the group, tap the group name, select Export Chat, and choose Without Media or With Media. Group chat exports include sender names for every message, and the formatter highlights system messages (member added, group name changed, etc.) automatically.
Can I export WhatsApp chats with media (photos, videos)?
WhatsApp offers two export options: "Without Media" (text-only .txt file) and "With Media" (.zip file containing text + media). The free version of the formatter handles .txt files. PRO users can upload the .zip file and the tool will parse both the text messages and the media attachments, showing media references in the export output.
What is the message limit for free exports?
The free version includes the first 100 messages from your chat export. You still get full access to all features: filters, statistics, and all five export formats. PRO removes this limit entirely, allowing you to export chats with thousands or even hundreds of thousands of messages.
Can I export WhatsApp chats to PDF?
The tool does not export PDF directly, but you can export as HTML and then use your browser's "Print to PDF" feature (Ctrl+P or Cmd+P) to save the styled chat as a PDF document. The HTML export includes print-friendly CSS for this purpose.
Does WhatsApp notify someone if I export our chat?
No. Exporting a chat is a local operation on your device. WhatsApp does not notify the other person or group members when you export a conversation. The export is entirely private and occurs on your phone.
What do I do if my chat is not parsing correctly?
The formatter supports both iOS and Android export formats and auto-detects the format. If you encounter issues, check that you selected the correct export option in WhatsApp. Make sure the file has a .txt extension. If problems persist, try exporting "Without Media" first, as media ZIP exports require the PRO version.
